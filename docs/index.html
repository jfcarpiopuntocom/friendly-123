    Your customers

    Every customer lives in a season, like your crops: 🌱 spring growing, ☀️ summer harvest, 🍂 fall cooling off, ❄️ winter asleep. The color tells you who to take care of and who to wake up.

    How does it work? ▸

    1. Add a customer once — they get a unique code and are placed in a “season” automatically based on how they buy.

    2. 🌱 spring = new/growing, ☀️ summer = your best customers, 🍂 fall = cooling off (win them back), ❄️ winter = inactive.

    3. Rate each customer's behavior — it builds a history only staff can see.

    4. Already have a customer list? Import it all at once from a CSV further down.

    Import your customer list from a file

    If you already have your customers in Excel or on your phone, save the file as CSV with two columns, name and phone, and upload it here. Each one gets their unique code automatically; duplicates (same name) are skipped.

    Commissions

    What you owe each promoter this month, calculated sale by sale — no spreadsheets, no surprises.

    How does it work? ▸

    1. Each partner/promoter shelf shows what they've sold this month and what you owe them.

    2. Tap “What if I sell more?” to simulate a bigger month and see the commission change live.

    3. When you pay, tap “Mark as paid” — it seals the month's sales and offers an itemized WhatsApp receipt.

    4. The ranking shows who's selling the most — and flags anyone who's gone quiet.
